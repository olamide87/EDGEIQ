# ADR 0005: Keep recommendations paper-only

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-07-16

## Context

Research quality, calibration, licensing, legal requirements, and operational safety
must be established before any real-money workflow could even be considered.

## Decision

EDGE IQ does not place wagers, automate sportsbook logins, or scrape sportsbook
websites. BET remains a paper recommendation label only.

## Consequences

The platform can validate projections, calibration, CLV, and paper performance while
avoiding execution risk. No model or dashboard may imply guaranteed profitability.
